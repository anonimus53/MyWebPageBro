The Forest needs your Help!

We are losing forests at a rate of 27 football fields a minute.
That is a startling number.
But the biggest problem for the world’s forests is not the outright clearing of forests, known as deforestation.
It is degradation. Degraded forests are a problem, as they reduce their efficiency in keeping air and water clean,
in providing wildlife with shelter and food, or in capturing carbon.

4 Types of trees that are in danger.

Soaring coast redwoods
Capacious American chestnuts
Elegant black ash
Gnarled whitebark pine
